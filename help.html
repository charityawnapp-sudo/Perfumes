Help Center

Frequently asked questions and support information.

Shipping Information

Orders are processed within 24 hours and delivered in 3-7 business days depending on destination.

Return Policy

Returns are accepted within 14 days for unopened products in original packaging with receipt.

How to Order

Browse products, click Buy Now, review your cart, then place your order with Cash on Delivery.

Can I track my order?

Yes. Go to the Orders page to see your order ID, date, total amount, and included items.

Do you offer same-day delivery in Bahrain?

Same-day delivery is available for selected areas when the order is placed before 1:00 PM.

How can I choose the right perfume?

Open the perfume details page to review ingredients and notes before adding the item to cart.

Can I cancel an order?

You can cancel before dispatch by contacting us through WhatsApp or phone during working hours.
